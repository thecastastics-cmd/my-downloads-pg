🏃‍♂️ Castastics On The Run!

Welcome to the ultimate endless runner experience! Castastics On The Run! Is a fast-paced, high-stakes mobile adventure where your reflexes are the only thing standing between you and a "Crashed!" screen.

🎮 Game Features - Massive Character Roster: Unlock and play as over 275 unique characters! From Commons to Ultra-Rares, build your collection and find your favorite runner.

Action-Packed Gameplay: Test your skills with smooth mobile controls—Jump over obstacles, Slide under barriers, and weave through traffic to keep your run alive.

Epic Villain Encounters: Watch out! You aren't alone on the road. Three dangerous villains are on the prowl, ready to attack and end your streak at any moment.

Powerful Boosts: Level up your run by collecting game-changing power-ups:

🧲 Magnet: Pull in every coin in sight without moving an inch.

🛡️ Shield: Protect yourself from a single crash.

✨ Multiplier: Double your score gain and climb the leaderboard faster.

Daily Rewards: Loyalty pays off! Log in every 24 hours to claim your Daily Reward of 1,000 coins to help unlock that next legendary character.

High Score Mastery: Track your "Best Score" and "Bank" total as you compete to become the ultimate Castastics Master.

🕹️ How to Play

Open the game, you can also skip the intro to quickly get into the action!

Hit "RUN!" to start.

Swipe left, right, up and down to move, jump, and slide.

Collect coins to spend in the shop and unlock new faces!

Take control of a massive universe featuring over 400 unique characters! Dodge obstacles, collect items, and outrun the chaos in this high-speed mobile runner developed entirely by David Artsy.
